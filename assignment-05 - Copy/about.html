Arnold Lobel was born in Los Angeles, CA but was raised in Schenectady, NY. Lobel did a lot of reading in the library when he was younger. He attened Pratt Institute in Brooklyn and after he graduated got married to another childrens auhtor he met in school, Anita Kempler. After college as he couldnt support himself just as a childrens author he worked in advertising and trade magazines. Lobel sadly passed away December 4, 1987.
